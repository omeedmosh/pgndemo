Bear in Water

Course: High School

This piece was made while taking a high school ceramics course. I was tasked with using new clay techniques to create a creative sculpture. I have always been fascinated by bears and chose to present one in a way that displays both the dangerous and delightful aspects of the beast. As it glides through the water, its beauty can be admired, but it can also be seen as a hunter ready to charge after its prey.
