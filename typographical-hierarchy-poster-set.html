Typographical Hierarchy Poster Set

Course: Typography I

This assignment pushed me to gain a deeper understanding of typographical hierarchy and how to effectively place text on a plane. I was tasked with creating a poster for an unusual word and its definition, and I chose the word Eleemosynary. The project required me to produce three versions of the poster: the first using a single style and point size, the second with multiple point sizes, and the third with both variation in point size and style. Throughout the project, I enjoyed the challenge of working within the limits of a frame and learned to interact with white space and edges to achieve a successful design.
