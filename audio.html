Breathe

Course: Digital Studio

This was an audio project dedicated to capturing the essence of a movie scene. I chose a scene from the film Interstellar, a sci-fi thriller that is set in space. The scene is filled with high intensity and anciety, so I attempted to capture that by adjusting typical daily noses, such as the sound of a skateboard, into deep, rythmic tones. My goal was to engulf the listener into a cloud of deep sounds until they felt like they were inside the scene itself.
