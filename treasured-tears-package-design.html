Treasured Tears Package Design

Course: Word and Image I

This assignment tasked us with creating an icon set based on a specific theme. Familiar with medical iconography and its common lack of personality and positivity, I chose to develop a set of my own that embodied a more lighthearted characteristic while still maintaining clear meanings. Hopefully, those who experience it in a medical setting would find it beneficial for their experience.
