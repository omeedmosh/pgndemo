Treasured Tears Package Design

Course: Word and Image I

This assignment required us to create product packaging for a fictional retail product. The theme assigned to me was melancholy, and I developed Treasured Tears, a time capsule designed to store sad memories and help people overcome sorrow through deep reflection and mindfulness. To convey the product's theme, I chose off-tones of gold and blue that created a melancholic aura. Additionally, I utilized a sans serif font and abstract imagery to project a minimal and modern feeling for the design.
